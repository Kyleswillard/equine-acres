Knox Butte Stables, formerly Knox Butte Equine Acres, is the premier horse facility in the Mid-Valley.

We offer a family friendly, drama free place for you and your horse. Come check us out. Our goal is for you to enjoy your time at our facility. The care of your horse is our commitment. We offer 3 levels of care: self, partial and full. Each can be adjusted to your needs. We have a 60x200 indoor arena, large outdoor riding area, hot water wash rack, round pen, large turnout pastures, hay storage and much more.
