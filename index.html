You couldn’t ask for a better site, we sit on a beautiful 90 acres in a central location; we are a couple of miles from Albany, close to Jefferson, and right down the road from Lebanon. It couldn’t be placed any better. All of our horses are under 24-hour video surveillance and have people on sight 24/7 in order to ensure the safest environment for the horses and our boarders. Our barn is not open to the public, and only our boarders and their guests are allowed on the premises. All of our boarders have access to our seasonal 100x300 outdoor arena, a large round pen, all 80 acres of our field, pond, and creek, and our 200x60 indoor arena with a mixed footing of dirt, sand, and pea gravel.

All of our members have access to the barn from 9 am to 9 pm every day all year long. For those who supply your own feed, we have a large hay barn for storage with limited storage in front of stalls. All shavings are included for our boarders and have access to plenty of wheelbarrows. We have tack rooms in each alley for tack storage and grooming tools. Every stall has its own water spigot or automatic water. There are 7 large pastures, designed for routine turnouts for all horses and we have a large parking area for boarders and guests and we do have limited trailer parking for an additional fee.

Here at Knox Butte Stables, we strive for consistency in the care of your horse(s). We recommend all of our prospective boarders to do at least one of our levels of partial care. Knowing all of the horses are fed twice a day at the same time provides the best care for all of the horses here. We offer limited self-care as it is hard for people to come in twice a day, every day as life is busy and that puts less stress on our boarders. With that here are the levels of care we offer:
